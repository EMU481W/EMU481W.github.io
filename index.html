Featured Capstone Projects

CineMatch

Tired of spending your whole movie night scrolling through options instead of actually watching something? CineMatch learns your tastes by letting you rate movies you’ve seen—and instantly recommends new titles you’ll love. Discover the right movie faster, save favorites for later, and make every movie night effortless and fun!

Visit CineMatch

Vybe

Tired of sending Spotify links to friends who only use YouTube? Stop letting platform barriers kill the music. Vybe bridges the gap between streaming services, allowing you and your friends to create shared groups, view each other’s playlists, and build the ultimate combined soundtrack regardless of where you listen. With features like "Song of the Day" and cross-platform exports, staying on the same wavelength has never been easier. Join us to see how we’re uniting music lovers, one track at a time.

Visit Vybe

BookBuddy

Meet BookBuddy! Your first choice for virtual book storage and searching! Have you ever wanted to be able to keep track of all of the books you've read and received user catered suggestions for your next read? Then, welcome to BookBuddy! Here you can keep track your digital and physical library in one place! Additionally, BookBuddy makes it easy to transfer your old libraries from other applications and integrate it with your new digital storage solution here!

Visit BookBuddy

Planit

What if planning a trip with friends was actually fun? Meet Planit—the travel-itinerary app that turns group chaos into adventure-ready clarity. Explore others trip itineraries for inspiration, create your very own trip itinerary, share with your friends, add activities, and watch your trip come together in real time! No more endless group chats. No more confusion. Just smooth, collaborative trip planning that gets everyone hyped for what’s next.

Visit Planit
